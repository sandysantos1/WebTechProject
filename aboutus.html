About Us

Welcome to La Paz that is dedicated to the amazing world of El Salvador coffee! There is a passion about all the aspects of El Salvadors coffee culture, history, and traditional coffee cultivation to the very detailed process of producing the best quality. Our goal is to be a resource for anyone interested in learning more about this unique aspect of Salvadoran culture, from coffee lovers to culture fanatics. We will explore the history, from its beginning to what it is present day significance in the regions culture and economy. Also, will be diving into the process of growing, harveting, and process of coffee beans. Highliting the characteritics and flavors that make it special. Join the journey with us at La Paz!

Creator

Hi, my name is Sandy Santos and I am the creator of this website. I am originally from Houston, Texas but my family background is Latin American. Both my parents were born and raised in El Salvador. They've told me many stories of their times back at home, but something that always stuck to me was the times they talked about how good the coffee is. My dad has told me stories of when he would work in these plantations picking coffee beans and whole process of how they are made. When I get the chance to visit my parents home country it's an amazing experience every time. Visiting gave me a chance of seeing the actual coffee plantations. When seeing the acres of land filled a thought always popped into my head. Why isn't Salvadorean coffee beans more popular?
