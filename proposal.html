Project Proposal

About

This will be a project based on my family's home country El Salvador and its coffee production. I will be talking about the process, history, taste, etc.

Objective

My objective is to bring light to this type of coffee. Coffee is one of my favirote drinks at any time, but when going to coffee places I never see my familys home country coffee being used. Having traveled there a few times in my life I can say the coffee production there is huge, so why is it not more popular in the states?

Target Audience

I would say my target audiience will be people that are coffee lovers that like trying new coffee. Also, people that like learning about a new culture and what the country specializes in.

Content

The content I would include on the site would be a description on what the website is about, the production and exports, the history, the types of coffee, the quality, and the importance of coffee to El Salvador.
